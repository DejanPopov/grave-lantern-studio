Our Story

About Us

Origin Story

Why We Exist

Grave Lantern Studio was born from a simple but stubborn belief: that small, passionate teams can create games that resonate deeply — games that linger in your memory long after the credits roll.

We were tired of games that prioritized spectacle over substance. We wanted to build experiences built on atmosphere, storytelling, and emotion. So we did.

"The best horror isn't what you see. It's what you imagine."

What We Stand For

Our Values

These aren't just words on a page. They're the principles that guide every decision we make.

🕯️

Atmosphere First

Every element — art, sound, design — serves the atmosphere. If it doesn't contribute to the feeling, it doesn't belong.

❤️

Made with Care

We don't ship half-baked products. Every release is something we're genuinely proud of, crafted with patience and attention to detail.

🌱

Always Learning

We're never done growing. Every project teaches us something new, and we carry those lessons forward.

🤝

Community Driven

Our players aren't just customers — they're collaborators. Their feedback shapes our games in meaningful ways.

🔓

Transparency

We document our journey openly — the wins and the struggles. No smoke and mirrors. Just honest game development.

🎮

Fun is Non-Negotiable

At the end of the day, games should be fun to make AND to play. If we're not enjoying the process, something's wrong.
