Origin Story

Why We Exist

Grave Lantern Studio was born from a simple but stubborn belief: that small, passionate teams can create games that resonate deeply — games that linger in your memory long after the credits roll.

We were tired of games that prioritized spectacle over substance. We wanted to build experiences built on atmosphere, storytelling, and emotion. So we did.

"The best horror isn't what you see. It's what you imagine."
